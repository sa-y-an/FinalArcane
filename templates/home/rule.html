Rules

Know the rules,So that you can break them effectively

Round 1 consists of a total of 7 questions carrying 15 points each.

In round 1, you can not skip a question to move forward to the next question.

Questions of round 1 will contain hints.

Requesting for hints will result in the lowering of points obtainable from that particular question by 5.

At the end of round 1 there is a bonus question of 40 marks.

Round 2 consists of 10 questions carrying 20 marks each.

You can navigate through all questions in round 2. There are no such restrictions as there were in round 1.

Answer every question of rounds 1 and 2 in the specified input format only.

The one who has the maximum points at the end of the quiz will be the winner. In case of a tie, the earlier the submission, the higher the ranking.

Good Luck
